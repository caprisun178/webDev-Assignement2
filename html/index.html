Introduction

I love food! But I really love good food. The point of this blog is to highlight some of my favorite spots here on campus. Let me know if you think they are hits or misses!

Don

Simple, booth-lined Japanese restaurant specializing in hot entrees like donburi, teriyaki & ramen.

Chipotle

Chipotle Mexican Grill, Inc., often known simply as Chipotle, is an American multinational chain of fast casual restaurants specializing in bowls, tacos, and Mission burritos made to order in front of the customer. As of December 2025, Chipotle has 4,000 locations.

Slices By Tony

Hand-tossed Italian slices and fan-favorite pies, made fresh daily with simple ingredients and a neighborhood, college-friendly vibe.
